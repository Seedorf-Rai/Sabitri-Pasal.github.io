About Us

Welcome to Sabitri Pasal, your delectable gateway to the authentic flavors of Nepal, right here in the heart of Dharan. We are a small, family-owned restaurant passionate about bringing you the true essence of Nepalese cuisine through our mouthwatering noodle delicacies.
Our specialty lies in the art of crafting tantalizing thukpa, a beloved noodle dish that warms the soul and delights the taste buds. With each spoonful of our carefully prepared thukpa, you'll savor the authentic blend of spices, fragrant herbs, and wholesome ingredients that make it an absolute must-try.
In addition to our scrumptious thukpa, we also serve an array of other Nepalese delights to further tantalize your palate. Delight in the savory goodness of our momo, tender dumplings filled with a medley of succulent flavors. Or dive into the irresistible allure of our chowmein, where stir-fried noodles are brought to life with a symphony of tastes and textures.
Beyond our culinary creations, Sabitri Pasal is a warm and welcoming space where you can truly immerse yourself in cool hospitality. Our friendly and attentive nature will ensure that your dining experience is not only a feast for the senses but also an opportunity to feel like a part of our extended family.
Whether you're a seasoned traveler seeking a taste of nostalgia or a curious foodie looking for a new culinary adventure, this is the place to be. Join us as we take you on a flavorful journey through the heart of Nepal, one bowl of thukpa at a time.
Come and experience the taste of tradition, the warmth of family, and the joy of savoring authentic Nepalese noodles. We look forward to welcoming you with open arms and serving you an unforgettable dining experience in Dharan, Nepal.
